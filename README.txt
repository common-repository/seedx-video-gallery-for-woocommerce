== Description ==
SeedX Video Gallery for WooCommerce allows you to have video on your product page for e-commerce sites created with the WooCommerce plugin. This plugin replaces the default WooCommerce gallery with a custom gallery, where you can use both images and video. The plugin is quite simple to use and starts working after installation. It doesn't require any additional settings. After SeedX Video Gallery for WooCommerce is installed and activated, you will see an additional input field in the right sidebar of product edit page (admin side). Just click on "Add Video" button, choose a video from your Media Library or upload a new one from your computer, click on the "Update" button to save changes for your product pages and new slider with images and video will appear at your product page.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the entire seedx-video-gallery-for-woocommerce folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Which video format could i use for this plugin? =
You can use all video formats that are supported by the current version of your WordPress installation.
